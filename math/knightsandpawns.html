Knights and Pawns

A...game?

The game is played on a 3x3 board

At the beginning, there be a knight, a pawn, or nothing on each square

On each turn, you can "capture" a piece.
Captures work like in chess, where pawns capture diagonally, while knights capture in a 2x1 L shape.

The game can be considered "won" if there's only 1 piece left. Can you win the above configuration?
